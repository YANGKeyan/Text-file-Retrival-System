SINGAPORE: Police on Friday (Dec 6) said they are appealing for information on the whereabouts of a 14-year-old girl who has been missing for two weeks.

Cai Xin Hui Valerie was last seen around Lorong Napiri in Hougang at about 10.30pm on Nov 22, police said in a news release.

Anyone with information can call the police hotline at 1800 255 0000 or submit the information online. All information will be kept confidential, said police.
